Popular routes

Where people go

🐖 Swimming Pigs, Big Major Cay

The world-famous Exuma pigs. Half-day round-trip from Nassau by seaplane, or full-day by yacht.

🦎 Iguana Beach, Allan Cay

Hand-feed the protected Bahamian rock iguanas. Best combined with a sandbar stop.

🤿 Thunderball Grotto

Snorkel inside the cathedral cave from the James Bond film. Light streams through the ceiling at midday.

🏖️ Rose Island Sandbar

Stand in waist-deep turquoise water 20 minutes from Nassau. Lunch on the sand.

🍹 Arawak Cay (Fish Fry)

Pre-yacht conch fritters and sky juice. We can arrange pickup at the dock.

🌅 Blue Lagoon Island

Private beach, dolphin encounters (optional), perfect half-day family trip.

FAQ

Common questions

How much does a yacht charter cost?

It varies — half-day cruises start around $X, full-day Exuma trips $X–$Y, week-long charters $Z+. Send us your dates and group size and we'll quote you the realistic range from our captains.

Do I need a license or experience?

No — every charter is fully crewed. You just bring the attitude and the swimsuits.

Can you accommodate dietary needs?

Yes. Tell us in the inquiry form — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, allergies, no problem. We brief the captain's provisioning.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms are set by each captain and shared with your quote. We always recommend trip insurance for weather-sensitive bookings.

How does payment work?

We don't take payment. You pay the captain or operator directly — bank transfer, credit card, or cash. We earn a commission from the operator after your trip is complete.
