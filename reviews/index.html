Review guidelines

Keeping it real

Who can leave a review?

Anyone who booked a trip through Bahamas Adventure Guides. Reviews from guests who didn't book through us are not accepted — this keeps feedback tied to real experiences.

Are reviews edited or filtered?

No. We publish every review we receive, positive or negative, as long as it follows these guidelines. We don't edit for tone, length, or content.

What's not allowed?

Hate speech, personal attacks on crew members, false statements about safety or legality, and spam. Reviews violating these rules are removed — everything else stays up.

Can I update or remove my review?

Contact us and we'll update or remove your review within 48 hours. No questions asked.
