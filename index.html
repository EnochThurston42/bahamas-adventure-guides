Common questions

Planning a Bahamas trip? Start here.

What types of boat rentals are available in Nassau?

Yacht charters, private boat charters, dinner cruises, and jet ski rentals. Options range from half-day trips to full-day excursions including the Exuma cays. Each listing shows exact capacity and what's included.

How do I book?

Contact us through the inquiry form. Tell us what you want — date, group size, and the kind of experience you're after. We'll send back options and a quote within 24 hours. No booking fees.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

No deposit needed. Quotes are free. Payment is made directly with the operator once you confirm. We don't hold your money.

Can I visit the swimming pigs from Nassau?

Yes. Full-day charters to the Exuma cays include stops at Pig Beach, sandbars, and snorkeling spots. These trips book out early — give us at least a week's notice.

Is hotel pickup included?

Hotel pickup and drop-off can be arranged for most charters and excursions. Let us know where you're staying and we'll include it in the quote.

What's the best time of year for a Bahamas boat trip?

November through April has calm seas and comfortable temperatures. Summer (May–October) is hotter but water visibility is excellent for snorkeling. Year-round is possible.
