How it works

The better way to book the Bahamas.

We built Bahamas Adventure Guides because we got tired of watching visitors get quoted double by booking engines. There's a better way — and it starts with talking to the actual captain.

Why we exist

The booking engine tax.

If you've ever searched "Nassau yacht charter" or "Exuma boat tour," you've seen it — the same trip listed on five different sites at five different prices, each one with a 15–25% "service fee" tacked on at checkout.

That fee doesn't go to the captain who runs your trip. It goes to a website you've never heard of, headquartered somewhere in another country, that scraped the operator's photos and put a margin on top.

We've watched it happen for years. So we built something different.

How we work.

You tell us what you want. We match you with the right captain from a small, vetted network of operators we know personally — most of them we've used ourselves or sent our own families to.

You pay them directly — no checkout surprises, no hidden fees. We earn from the operator, after your trip is done, never from you.

Because we never take payment from you, our only incentive is to send you to operators who'll treat you right. Otherwise we'd never hear from you again.

What we don't do.

We don't list every operator in the Bahamas. We don't pretend to be impartial. We don't take referral fees from shady operators just because they showed up first in our inbox.

If a captain isn't good enough for our families, they aren't good enough for yours.
