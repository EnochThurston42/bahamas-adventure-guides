FAQ

Common questions

How much does a yacht charter cost?

It varies by vessel, duration, and destination — every trip is custom-quoted. The Clicquot is berthed at Paradise Harbour Club & Marina, Nassau — a 48' crewed yacht. Send us your dates and group size and we'll connect you with the captain for a fair, direct quote.

Do I need a license or experience?

No — every charter on the Clicquot is fully crewed with a licensed captain and first mate. Just bring your swimwear and sunscreen.

Can you accommodate dietary needs?

Yes. Tell us in the inquiry form — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, allergies, no problem. We brief the captain's provisioning. The Clicquot has a full kitchen for chef-prepared meals on board.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms are set by the captain and shared with your quote. We always recommend trip insurance for weather-sensitive bookings in the Bahamas.

How does payment work?

We don't take payment. You pay the captain or operator directly — bank transfer, credit card, or cash. We earn a commission from the operator after your trip is complete.

What's on board the Clicquot?

4 cabins, Starlink WiFi, full kitchen, AC throughout, and enough water gear to keep everyone busy (see full list above). The captain handles the rest.

Ready to charter the Clicquot?

Tell us your dates and group size and we'll send back a real quote from the captain at Paradise Harbour Club & Marina within 24 hours.
