Signature experiences

Two ways to boat charters

Full-Day Experience

6–8 hours. Swimming pigs at Athol Island, Iguana Cay for the rock iguanas, and snorkeling the underwater sculpture garden. Beach lunch included.

  • Captain + first mate
  • Lunch and drinks
  • Snorkel gear included
  • All fuel and customs fees

Half-Day Sandbar Run

4 hours. Rose Island or Sandy Toes. Stand in waist-deep turquoise water, eat conch salad on the sand.

  • Captain + crew
  • Drinks & snacks
  • Tubes & float mats
  • Perfect for families
